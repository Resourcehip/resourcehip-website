# Get your product rated

Generic category ratings use conservative defaults. Your actual product is almost certainly better than the baseline — a Verified Rating proves it.

## The problem with generic data

Every product category on Resourcehip has a generic baseline score built from publicly available data. Where evidence is missing, the methodology assumes the worst reasonable case. That means generic scores are deliberately low.

Your product's real materials, supply chain, and end-of-life story will tell a different picture. A Verified Rating replaces those conservative assumptions with your actual data.

## What you get

- **A published Verified Rating** on resourcehip.com with your product name, brand, and score
- **HIP Mark eligibility** — if your product scores 6.0 or above, it qualifies for the HIP Mark badge (Standard, Silver, or Gold tier)
- **Independent third-party assessment** — not a self-declaration, but a scored, evidence-based review by an independent assessor
- **ESG and procurement documentation** — a citable, stable reference for ESG reporting, tender responses, and retailer onboarding

## How it works

1. **Submit your product details** via our intake form or by email
2. **We scope and invoice** within 3 working days
3. **Assessment** — our assessors review your evidence dossier against the HIP methodology (2-4 weeks from complete submission)
4. **Publication** — on payment confirmation, we publish the rating and issue the HIP Mark licence where eligible
