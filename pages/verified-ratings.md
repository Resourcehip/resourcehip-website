# Get a Verified Rating

Resourcehip publishes two types of rating: **Category Ratings** and **Verified Ratings**. Category ratings assess a whole product category using publicly available data — no specific manufacturer is named. Verified ratings are different.

## What is a Verified Rating?

A Verified Rating assesses a specific product from a named manufacturer, using evidence submitted directly by that manufacturer. Where a Category Rating uses conservative assumptions based on publicly available data, a Verified Rating draws on the actual materials, supply chain, and end-of-life data for a specific product.

Verified ratings almost always score higher than the category baseline — because real data about what a product actually contains and how it is made tells a better story than conservative assumptions.

## Who is it for?

Verified Ratings are for:

- **Product manufacturers** who want their specific product assessed on its actual materials and construction, rather than against a conservative category-wide estimate.
- **Brand owners** who want to demonstrate — with independently assessed evidence — that their product outperforms the generic category baseline.

## Why it matters: the HIP Mark

The HIP Mark is Resourcehip's consumer-facing quality badge. It has three tiers:

| Tier | HIP Score required | Additional requirement | Rating type required |
|------|--------------------|------------------------|----------------------|
| HIP Mark Standard | 6.0+ | — | Verified |
| HIP Mark Silver | 7.5+ | — | Verified |
| HIP Mark Gold | 9.0+ | Regenerative Index ≥ +6.0 | Verified |

**A Verified Rating is required for all HIP Mark tiers.** Generic category ratings are not eligible. This ensures the badge reflects real, independently verified product data — not an estimate.
