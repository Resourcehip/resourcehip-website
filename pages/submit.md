# Suggest a Product

We publish independent HIP Score ratings for everyday consumer products — from kettles to cordless drills. Every rating we add helps shoppers make better-informed choices about the long-term material impact of what they buy.

## Tell us what to rate next

If there's a product category you'd like us to rate, we'd love to hear from you. Drop us an email with your suggestion and we'll add it to our ratings queue.

[Suggest a product category →](mailto:hello@resourcehip.com?subject=Product%20suggestion)

We read every message. We can't guarantee a timeline, but popular requests move up the queue.

---

## What makes a good suggestion?

We rate **product categories** rather than individual brands — for example, "cordless drills" rather than "Brand X Model Y". The best suggestions are:

- everyday products that many people buy
- categories where materials and supply chains are worth scrutinising
- things you genuinely wish you had better information about before purchasing

If you're a manufacturer and want a **Verified Rating** for a specific product, that's a separate process — [get in touch](mailto:hello@resourcehip.com?subject=Verified%20rating%20enquiry) and we can explain how it works.
