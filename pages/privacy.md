## 1. What data we collect and why

### Analytics

We use **Cloudflare Web Analytics** — a cookieless service that collects no personal data. Only aggregated, anonymised traffic data is collected (page views, referrer, approximate country). No cookie is placed on your device. No individual tracking occurs. No consent is required.

### Contact enquiries

If you contact us by email or through a contact form, we collect your name and email address to respond to your enquiry. We retain correspondence for 12 months, then delete it. **Legal basis:** Legitimate interest.

### Business and licensing enquiries

If you enquire about HIP Mark licensing or a product rating, we collect your name, business name, email, and enquiry details. We retain this for 3 years. **Legal basis:** Pre-contractual steps / legitimate interest.

### What we do not collect

We do not collect payment card details, sensitive personal data, or government identifiers. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing tools. We do not sell your data.
