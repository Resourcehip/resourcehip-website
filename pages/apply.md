# Apply for Verified Assessment

A Verified Rating assesses your specific product using the evidence you provide — materials composition, supply chain provenance, repairability, and end-of-life pathways. Unlike a category rating, it reflects what your product actually contains and how it is actually made.

Verified products that score 6.0 or above are eligible to licence the **HIP Mark**, Resourcehip's consumer-facing quality badge.

[Learn how Verified Ratings work →](/verified-ratings)

---

## Before you apply

To assess your product we will need:

- **Company and product details** — legal entity name, brand name, product model name and SKU
- **Product category** — for example, "cordless drill" or "stainless steel water bottle"
- **Evidence dossier** — materials composition data, supply chain documentation, repairability and end-of-life information. A submission template is provided after enquiry.
- **Contact details** — the person leading the submission

You do not need a complete dossier to get in touch. If you are unsure whether your evidence is sufficient, send what you have and we will let you know what is needed.

## What happens next

1. **We review your enquiry** and confirm whether your evidence is sufficient to proceed.
2. **We send a submission template** covering all seven HIP dimensions.
3. **You complete the dossier** and return it to us.
4. **Assessment** — your product is scored by our analysts and you receive a full dimension breakdown.
5. **HIP Mark licence** (if eligible) — products scoring 6.0+ can licence the HIP Mark for use in packaging and marketing.
