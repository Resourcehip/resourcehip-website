## How ratings are produced — AI transparency

Every HIP Score on this website is produced by an AI scoring pipeline and **reviewed by a human assessor before publication**. No rating is ever published automatically.

**The AI pipeline runs locally on Resourcehip's own hardware. No product data is sent to external cloud AI services.**

| Component | Role |
|-----------|------|
| Model — qwen3.5:35b | Handles all scoring (all 7 dimensions) and all consumer prose generation |
| Temperature | 0.1 — consistent and reproducible outputs |
| Human review | Every rating reviewed and approved by a human before publication |

The AI produces a draft. The human decides what is published.

### Why we use AI

AI allows Resourcehip to apply the same scoring rubric consistently across many product categories. The combination of AI consistency and human oversight produces more reliable results than purely manual assessment, where individual judgement varies.

### EU AI Act note

The EU AI Act (Article 50) introduces transparency obligations for certain AI-generated content from August 2026. This notice is consistent with those obligations. Our pipeline does not use any high-risk AI system as defined by the Act.

## What we do not do

- No advertising cookies or tracking pixels of any kind
- No Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or behavioural advertising trackers
- No sale of data to third parties
- No fully automated decisions — all ratings are human-approved
- No transmission of product data to external cloud AI services
- No AI-generated fabricated data — scores are based on real data inputs
