# Is this product good for the planet?

Resourcehip rates everyday consumer products on how resilient, responsibly sourced, and long-lived they are. Every rating is free, independent, and reviewed by a human before publication.

## What is the HIP Score?

The HIP Score (Human Impact Profile Score) is a number from 0 to 10 that summarises how resilient a product is from a materials and supply chain perspective. It is not a carbon footprint — it focuses on whether the materials a product depends on are abundant or at risk, whether they are sourced responsibly, and whether the product can be repaired and recovered at end of life.

## Why do scores look low?

Our generic ratings start from conservative defaults — where data is absent, we assume the worst reasonable case. The gap between that baseline and what a product can actually demonstrate is called **headroom**. When a manufacturer submits verified data, the same scoring pipeline produces a more accurate picture.

Low scores are not a criticism of any specific product. They reflect the category-wide reality of consumer manufacturing today.

## The HIP Mark

Products that earn a verified HIP Score can carry the HIP Mark — a consumer-facing badge with three tiers:

- **Standard** — HIP Score 6.0 or above
- **Silver** — HIP Score 7.5 or above
- **Gold** — HIP Score 9.0 or above

When you see the HIP Mark on a product, it means an independent assessor has verified the manufacturer's claims using the HIP methodology.
